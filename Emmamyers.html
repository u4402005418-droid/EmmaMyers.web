(Emma Myers is a noted American actress, Emma Elizabeth Myers, born on April 2, 2002, in Orlando, Florida, is an American actress. She was homeschooled and did not attend traditional school, developing creative interests and a strong love for film and television from a young age. She made her acting debut in 2010 as a child actress with a small role in the TV series The Glades, and in the same year appeared in the film Letters to God. After a break, she resumed acting in the 2020s with various TV and film roles, reaching international fame in 2022 by playing Enid Sinclair in the Netflix series Wednesday.)
